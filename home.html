HOME

Greetings from Danger Zone, I’m your host Ripper Ronton. This is an unofficial fan site for the cult sensation hit, Dungeon Crawler Carl. Here at Danger Zone, we strive to be your dungeon guide to all things DCC. In its current incarnation you will find the latest in DCC news, an introduction to our heroes, book summaries and audio book samples. Once the site has been fully constructed, you can expect to find information about DCC in other mediums ( the web comic, the tabletop RPG, Soundbooth Theatre's audio immersion, the new streaming series ), information about the author, links to DCC/DCC merch sites, and other fun stuff ( ringtones, trivia, fan essays and fanfic ).
